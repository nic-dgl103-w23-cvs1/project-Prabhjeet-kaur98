About Salon

Blush Salon and Spa is a beauty salon located in Courtenay, British Columbia, Canada. They offer a variety of services, including haircuts, hair coloring, hair extensions, manicures, pedicures, waxing, facials, and makeup application. The salon is known for its relaxing and welcoming atmosphere, as well as its skilled and experienced staff. Blush Salon and Spa uses high-quality products from top brands such as Schwarzkopf, Bumble and bumble, Dermalogica, and Jane Iredale. They strive to provide personalized and attentive service to each client, ensuring that they leave feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and satisfied with their experience.
